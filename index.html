About Me

I am a budding web developer that lives in Durham, NC. I love vivid colors, whimsy and little surprises where you wouldn't expect them. I have a great time experimenting and exploring. My goal is to bring all of these things to well thought out project for my clients that is not only easy to use, but pleasureable.
